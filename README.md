A Five Hair studio is currently facing some problems due to their old ways of booking
appointments. People have to wait in a long line for a long time, and the constant
misunderstanding about hairstyles between the barber and the customer. As a result, some of
the customers leave the barbershop with mixed feelings. Customers also often avoid saying
something when their hair is not being cut in their preferred style during the cutting process.
An online booking and hair consultation system can be a solution to the current
problem. The system will allow the customer to easily book their appointments on the
barbershop website, lowering waiting times. There will also be a feature where the customer
can share what kind of hair problems they are facing, and what kind of hairstyle they want
before getting a haircut. This helps barbers understand what the customer wants, making a
satisfactory experience from the user side and the barber. This change using the system can
